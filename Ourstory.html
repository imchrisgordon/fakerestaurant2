Our Story




Five guys, a band, a van, and the great Canadian North.
That's where our journey to the wonders of French Fries and gravy began.
During the dead of winter we set out on a journey to rock the masses.
Little did we know that it was our taste buds that were about to be rocked.
With the elements hammering down and the roads slick as hell.
We had decided to pull into an ominous yet inviting bed and breakfast,
that to our surprise was open.
As we entered, the heat from the wood burnig stove had warmed us almost instanly.
We were greeted by the kindest elderly couple we had ever incountered.
Now I know whart you're thinking, "This is how horror movies start",
They did wind up murdering our hunger with their heavenly combo of potatoes, gravy,and cheese curds.
And our bassist, but thats another story.
From that day forward we have strived to bring that warm hospitable feeling to you.
Minus the murder.
We hope you enjoy this dish as much as we have for these past ten years.
